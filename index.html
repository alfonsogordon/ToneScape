Privacy Policy – ToneScape

Effective Date: 27 March 2026
Developer: AG Audio
Contact: tonespaceapp@gmail.com

1. Overview

ToneScape is designed to be a simple, creative audio tool. Your privacy is important, and this app is built to operate without collecting personal data.

2. Data Collection

ToneScape does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

This includes:

  • No names, emails, or contact details
  • No location data
  • No usage tracking or analytics
  • No background data collection

3. Permissions

ToneScape may request basic device permissions required for core functionality, such as:

  • Audio output (to generate sound)

These permissions are used only for app functionality and never for data collection.

4. Third-Party Services

ToneScape does not use third-party services that collect user data, such as:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • External tracking tools

5. Data Security

Since ToneScape does not collect or store user data, there is no personal data at risk within the app.

6. Children’s Privacy

ToneScape does not knowingly collect any data from users, including children under 13. The app is safe to use without providing personal information.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
